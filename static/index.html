Welcome to Acoustix AI DAW! 🎧

Acoustix is a browser-powered AI music generator. Here is how you can create your first song in 4 easy steps:

1

Load the AI Engine

Wait for the AI Engine indicator in the header to turn green (AI Ready). The model will be cached in your browser for fast loading next time!

2

Design Loop Elements

Fill in the four properties (Instrument, Style, Mood, BPM) to define your loop, then press Generate Loop. The AI will output a perfect loop.

3

Arrange on the Timeline

Drag loops from your library into the tracks (Track 1 to Track 8). Drag a placed clip to move it, or grab either edge to trim or extend it with looped playback. Clips still snap to the grid based on your project tempo.

4

Listen and Export

Press the spacebar to play/pause, use M to mute a track, S to solo it, adjust volumes, toggle loop mode, and click Export Song to download your final WAV mixdown!
